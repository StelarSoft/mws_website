Who are we?

We are Marine World Services (MWS), and we strive to provide the best value proposition to your business. Be it domestic, regional, or global, we will cover your requirements. We are a Guyanese owned company and our goal is to ensure that we provide the best in shipping, logistics and marine services and we have the reach, resources, expertise, and the experience to deliver on that promise.

Our Services

Freight Services

Whatever your cargo, wherever it's heading, Marine World Solutions has got your freight forwarding needs covered with our comprehensive freight and logistics services by sea, by air and by land.

Clearing and Delivery

Wherever you go, Marine World Services is the freight forwarding company with the local know-how and the global resources to guide your shipments through the customs clearance process and send them on their way to their destination.

Marine Services

When your engaged in offshore exploration, construction, and production, laying submarine pipes or cables, or operating a rig or production platform, you need a marine services partner who can shoulder the load. Marine World Services has the know-how and equipment needed to make sure even the biggest jobs go as planned.

Agency Services

Speedy and well-coordinated husbandry services enable fast turnaround of vessels: optimized operational efficiency through minimal time spent alongside or at anchor.
